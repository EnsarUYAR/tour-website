Tour Overview

Sapadere Canyon! Sapadere Canyon has been formed by wind and water for a long time. The walk from the jeep to the canyon is about 700 meters, and the walking trail is built for easy transportation. During the walk you can admire the rugged walls of the canyon, the beautiful natural pools and breathe in the fresh air of the mountains. Natural pools consist of water flowing from beautiful mountains. It is also possible to swim in the natural pools. The temperature of the canyon is always cool, and the water does not exceed 12 c even in hot weather.
